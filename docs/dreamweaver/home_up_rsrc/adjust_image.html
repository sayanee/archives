Levels

 up
 
  • Go to Image > Adjustments > Levels

  • You can adjust the image's brightness and contrast by adjusting the shadows, midtones and highlights levels
  
     

Curves

 up
 
  • Go to Image > Adjustments > Curves

  • Curves is also used to adjust an image's levels
