Pen Tool

 up
 
  • Choose the Pen Tool to draw curves and straight lines

  • Click once to mark the first point and click again at a distance to draw a straight line
  • To draw a curve: Click at a point a drag away
  • Click at a position where you want the curve to end

  • Choose Add Anchor Point to add and achor for you to curve from that point
  • Drag from the centre of the anchor point to make a curve
