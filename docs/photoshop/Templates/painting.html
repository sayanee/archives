Editing an existing brush

 up
 
  • Select the Brush Tool
  • Select a Brush that you want to edit from the Brush Options Tool

  • Open up the Brush Palette

  • Edit it by adding more options and refining their adjustments

  • Close the Brush Palette. Click open the Brush list and Click the Circle Arrow

  • Choose New Brush
  • Name your new brush
  • You will find that your new brush is added at the bottom of the list of existing brushes
  
     

Creating Brush

 up
 
  • Select a portion of a graphic that you want to convert to Brush

  • Go to Edit> Define Brush

  • Name your Brush

  • Your new brush will be added below the rest of the brushes
  • Go to Brush Tool and Select your Brush. Paint with it and try out
