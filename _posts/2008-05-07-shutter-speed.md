<strong>Shutter Speed</strong> is the length of time the camera shutter is open and it is alos known as the exposure <strong>time</strong>.

Some factors affecting shutter speed:
<ol>
	<li>total exposure is propertional to exposure time/light reaching the image sensor of a DSLR</li>
	<li>faster shutter speed requires a larger aperture, and slower shutter speed requires a smaller aperture</li>
</ol>
