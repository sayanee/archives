<strong>Name</strong>: The Thinker (French: Le Penseur)
<strong>Object</strong>: Sculpture
<strong>Artist</strong>: Auguste Rodin
<strong>Date of creation</strong>: 1902
<strong>Style</strong>: This sculpture depicts a man sitting in deep ponderation. It is often used ot represent Philosophy.
<strong>Method</strong>: Bronze and Marble
<strong>Where to find it</strong>: Paris, Musée Rodin
