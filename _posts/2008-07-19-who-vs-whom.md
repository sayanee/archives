<strong>Who</strong>
<ul>
	<li>answers the subject of the question</li>
	<li>the answer of the question should be the object form of pronoun</li>
	<li>E.g. Who wrote the codes? He wrote!</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div>

<strong>Whom</strong>
<ul>
	<li>answers the object of the question</li>
	<li>the answer of the question should be the subject form of pronoun</li>
	<li>E.g. By whom was the window broken? By him!</li>
</ul>
