<strong>Depth of Field</strong> (DOF) is the portion of the photograph that is in focus as compared to the rest of the areas of the photo.

Factors that depends on DOF:
<ol>
	<li>Subject distance from the camera</li>
	<li>Focal length</li>
	<li>aperture</li>
</ol>
