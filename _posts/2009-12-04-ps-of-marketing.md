The [Ps of marketing](http://www.valuebasedmanagement.net/methods_marketing_mix.html)... The 4 Ps of marketing are:

1. Product
2. Place
3. Promotion
4. Price

The 5 Ps of marketing are:
1. Product
2. Place
3. Promotion
4. Price
5. People

The 7 Ps of marketing (Extended Marketing Mix of Booms and Bitner ) are:
1. Product
2. Place
3. Promotion
4. Price
5. People
6. Process
7. Physical Evidence
